1. Collect raw queries from free online sources

- StackOverflow
- Reddit
- Google Natural Questions
- HotpotQA
- Spider (NL questions only)

2. Apply your own routing labels

- small‑LLM → simple, short, low‑reasoning questions

3. Train a supervised classifier
You will experiment with at least two models, such as:
- Logistic Regression (TF‑IDF)
- BERT‑based classifier

4. Evaluate routing performance
You will measure:
- Accuracy
- Precision / Recall / F1
- Possibly AUROC
Your goal is to achieve F1 ≥ 60, which is realistic because simple vs complex queries are linguistically separable.
5. Perform dataset‑related experiments
Examples:
- Effect of dataset size
- Effect of class imbalance
- PCA vs no PCA
- Data augmentation (paraphrasing)
These match the assignment’s “research questions” requirement.
